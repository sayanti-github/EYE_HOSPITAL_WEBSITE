INFRASTRUCTURE

Haldia Eye Hospital is a prominent healthcare institution dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care services to the residents of Haldia and the surrounding regions. Established with a commitment to improving eye health and preventing vision-related issues, the hospital has grown significantly over the years.

Bed Capacity

Haldia Eye Hospital officially boasts a bed capacity of 10 beds, ensuring that patients receive the necessary post-operative care and observation. However,

to better serve the community, the hospital has expanded its capacity to accommodate up to 25 beds, allowing for more patients to receive timely treatment and care. Read less..

Operating Theater (OT)

The hospital is equipped with a state-of-the-art operating theater (OT) where surgical procedures, including cataract surgery and other eye surgeries,

Patient Cabins

Haldia Eye Hospital provides patient cabins, including separate male and female wards, to offer comfort and privacy to patients during their stay.

Doctor's Chambers

The hospital has two doctors' chambers, facilitating consultations and follow-up appointments with the medical professionals. This ensures that patients

Office

An administrative office is in place to manage patient records, appointments, billing, and overall hospital operations. It serves as the central hub for hospital administration.

Waiting Room

A comfortable and welcoming waiting room is available for patients and their families. It offers a tranquil space for patients to relax before and after their appointments or procedures.

About Our Hospital

The Lions Eye Hospital was founded on October 2, 1980, through the dedicated efforts and initiative of the members of the Lions Club in Haldia. It was originally located near the old State Bank in the CPT Market area of Haldia.
