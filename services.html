Our Services

Lions Eye hospitals play a crucial role in promoting eye health, preventing vision-related issues, and providing advanced medical care for various eye conditions

  • Eye Examinations

  • Cataract Surgery

  • Refractive Surgery

  • Corneal Services

Eye Examinations

Eye hospitals offer comprehensive eye examinations to assess vision and detect eye problems. These exams include visual acuity tests, refraction tests, and assessments for common eye conditions like glaucoma, cataracts, and macular degeneration.

Cataract Surgery

Cataract surgery is a common procedure performed in eye hospitals to remove clouded lenses and restore clear vision. Advanced techniques, such as phacoemulsification, are often used for this purpose

Refractive Surgery

Eye hospitals provide refractive surgery options, such as LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis), PRK (Photorefractive Keratectomy), and implantable lenses, to correct vision problems like nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism.

Retinal Care

Retinal conditions like diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachment, and age-related macular degeneration are diagnosed and treated in specialized retinal clinics within eye hospitals. Treatments may include laser therapy, intravitreal injections, or surgery.

Corneal Services

Eye hospitals offer corneal transplantation and treatment for conditions affecting the cornea, such as keratoconus, corneal ulcers, and dystrophies.

About Our Hospital

The Lions Eye Hospital was founded on October 2, 1980, through the dedicated efforts and initiative of the members of the Lions Club in Haldia. It was originally located near the old State Bank in the CPT Market area of Haldia.
